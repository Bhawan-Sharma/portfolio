About

Hey stranger, how’s it going?
I’m Ruchika, but the people who know me call me Ru. I can start by telling you all the important things that I think you want to hear. You know, things like; I have a passion for fashion, digital marketing caught my eye, and I’m a curious soul, etc., etc.
But that’s Ruchika. I’m here to talk about Ru. And honestly, that conversation is far more interesting than the typical stuff you’ve probably heard thousands of people say before.
So back to digital marketing. Other than it just being a fast pace, ever-changing, and super fun job, it honestly allowed me to gain knowledge into platforms that I spend probably 90% of my time on. And the knowledge that I could use to my advantage.
But what truly made me fall in love with digital marketing, specifically the social media realm, was the fact that I got to sell a brand. I got to create its voice, its vibe, the whole aesthetic, and pretty much everything else. It pushed me to learn. It pushed me to want to learn more.
And the creativity behind it was a whole new level high that not even an acid trip could give you. Creating ideas that weren’t done before, content that would just hit the spot, thinking about every little intricate detail, and so much more. Now, if you’ve read this far, I expect you to call me Ru when we meet.
